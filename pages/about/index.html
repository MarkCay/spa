Zerobstacle Technologies was founded by Jong Bautista on April 30, 2019. On year 2020, Zerobstacle Technologies started developing mobile and web app. One of their early projects is the ‘Paperless System App’, which was used by the Palawan Council for Sustainable Development for their system. They also developed variety of web and mobile apps not only in Palawan, but outside of Palawan as well.
